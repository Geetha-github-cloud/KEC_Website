Life at KEC

Kongu Engineering College provides a vibrant and balanced student experience, focusing on both academics and extracurricular growth.
Academic Excellence: The college offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, including Centers of Excellence (CoEs) and a modern library with RFID facilities, ensuring students gain hands-on industry-relevant skills.
Facilities: Students enjoy comfortable hostel accommodations and access to well-maintained sports facilities.
Extracurricular Activities: With numerous clubs (technical, cultural, and leadership) and opportunities in sports, students can develop their interests beyond academics.
Career and Placements: The college has a strong placement record, with leading companies offering competitive packages to graduates.
Discipline and Culture: KEC emphasizes a disciplined and respectful environment, encouraging holistic development.
